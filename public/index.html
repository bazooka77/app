Pingchat FAQ

1. What is Pingchat? +

Pingchat is a peer-to-peer web chat application designed to provide fast, secure, and user-friendly communication directly from your browser.

2. Is Pingchat free to use? +

Yes, Pingchat is currently free to use. In the future, premium features will be introduced to enhance the user experience.

3. Do I need to create an account to use Pingchat? +

No! Pingchat allows you to chat without the hassle of creating accounts, ensuring a seamless and private experience.

4. Pingchat Coin Launch on Pump.fun +

What is the total supply of Pingchat Coin?
Pingchat Coin will have a fixed total supply of 100 million coins.

5. How will Pingchat Coin be launched? +

Pingchat Coin will have a fair launch exclusively on Pump.fun. There will be no ICO, ensuring equal opportunities for everyone to participate.

6. How can I trust that Pingchat Coin is secure? +

Pingchat is committed to transparency and fairness. Only 3-4% of the total supply will be held by the developers, ensuring no possibility of a rug pull. The remaining supply will be available for the community to acquire and trade.

7. How can I purchase Pingchat Coin during the launch? +

To participate in the fair launch:
1. Set up a Solana-compatible wallet (e.g., Phantom or Solflare).
2. Fund your wallet with Solana (SOL).
3. Visit Pump.fun on launch day, connect your wallet, and buy Pingchat Coins directly.

8. What can I use Pingchat Coin for? +

Pingchat Coin will integrate with the Pingchat ecosystem to:
- Unlock premium features.
- Reward community members and creators.
- Power community-driven initiatives.
